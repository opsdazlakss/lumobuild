## ‚öôÔ∏è System Overview

The application functions as a decentralized-serverless hybrid model, mirroring the efficiency of well-tuned mechanical assemblies.

### 1. **Infrastructure & Logic**
*   **Database (Firestore):** Selected for its flexible structure, similar to modular design in manufacturing.
*   **Security (RBAC):** Implemented strict access controls (Admin/Moderator/User) akin to safety protocols in industrial machinery.
*   **Synchronization:** Real-time listeners ensure all clients operate in lock-step, just like synchronized gears.

### 2. **Client-Side Structure**
*   **React 18:** Used for its component-based architecture, allowing for "part-swapping" and ease of maintenance.
*   **Optimized Rendering:** Focused on efficiency and minimizing waste (latency/memory), a core tenant of engineering processing.

### 3. **Communication Protocols**
*   **WebRTC & PeerJS:** Establishes direct Peer-to-Peer connections for voice/video, reducing central server load‚Äîefficient energy distribution.
*   **Cross-Platform Wrappers:** Electron (Windows) and Capacitor (Android) allow the system to operate in multiple environments without redesigning the core "engine."
